🗂️ Curriculum Planner (10 Weeks)

Here is a clean, printable, and minimal B1 Spanish Curriculum Planner with checkboxes, organized by weeks, skills, and goals.

You can mark progress using ☐ (not done) and ☑ (done).

📅 Week 1–2: Foundations Refresh

🔤 Grammar & Basics​

  • ☐ Review Presente
  • ☐ Review Pretérito Perfecto
  • ☐ Review Futuro perifrástico (ir a + infinitivo)
  • ☐ Practice basic connectors (pero, porque, aunque)

🧠 Vocabulary​

  • ☐ Daily routines
  • ☐ Personal information phrases

🗣 Speaking & Listening​

  • ☐ Listen to 2 beginner/B1 dialogues
  • ☐ Practice introducing yourself (1–2 minutes)

✍ Writing​

  • ☐ Write a short self‑introduction (80 words)

📅 Week 3–4: Core B1 Grammar

🔤 Grammar Focus​

  • ☐ Pretérito Indefinido
  • ☐ Pretérito Imperfecto
  • ☐ Contrast: indefinido vs imperfecto
  • ☐ Subjuntivo (presente) in basic triggers
    • ☐ Quiero que…
    • ☐ Es importante que…
    • ☐ Dudo que…
  • ☐ Pronouns:
    • ☐ Direct (lo, la, los, las)
    • ☐ Indirect (le, les → se)
    • ☐ Double pronouns (se lo, me la…)

🧠 Vocabulary​

  • ☐ Travel & holidays
  • ☐ Feelings & preferences

🗣 Speaking & Listening​

  • ☐ Describe a past trip (1–2 minutes)
  • ☐ Debate simple opinions using subjuntivo

✍ Writing​

  • ☐ Write a short narrative (100 words) in past tenses

📅 Week 5–6: Functional Language + Vocab

🔤 Grammar Focus​

  • ☐ Por vs Para
  • ☐ Ser vs Estar
  • ☐ Perífrasis verbales:
    • ☐ seguir + gerundio
    • ☐ acabar de + infinitivo
    • ☐ llevar + gerundio

🧠 Vocabulary​

  • ☐ Work & office
  • ☐ Technology
  • ☐ Health & wellbeing

🗣 Speaking & Listening​

  • ☐ Doctor/clinic role‑play
  • ☐ Work‑related dialogue practice

✍ Writing​

  • ☐ Write a formal or neutral work email (120 words)

📅 Week 7–8: Skills Training

📖 Reading​

  • ☐ Read 3 B1‑level articles
  • ☐ Identify connectors in texts

🔊 Listening​

  • ☐ Listen to 3 short podcasts/videos (5–10 min)
  • ☐ Take notes using bullet points

🗣 Speaking​

  • ☐ 4‑minute structured talk (describe + narrate + opinion)
  • ☐ Role-play at a restaurant or shop

✍ Writing​

  • ☐ Write 120–150 word description/opinion
  • ☐ Rewrite text for clarity (using connectors)

📅 Week 9–10: Exam Preparation

💬 Grammar Review​

  • ☐ Review all past tenses
  • ☐ Pronoun review
  • ☐ Subjuntivo basic triggers
  • ☐ Por vs para / ser vs estar

📝 Mock Exam Tasks​

  • ☐ Reading task
  • ☐ Listening task
  • ☐ Grammar task
  • ☐ Writing task
  • ☐ Speaking simulation (6–8 min)

🔁 Error Correction​

  • ☐ Make an “error list”
  • ☐ Create 10 Anki cards from mistakes
  • ☐ Redo 1 writing task applying corrections
